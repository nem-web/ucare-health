## Automatic Installation (Recommended)

### Chrome Browser
1. **Open the app** in Chrome browser on your Android phone
2. **Look for install prompt** - A banner will appear saying "Install Ucare Health app"
3. **Tap "Install"** - The app will be added to your home screen
4. **Open from home screen** - Tap the Ucare icon to launch as a native app

### Manual Installation

If the automatic prompt doesn't appear:

1. **Open Chrome menu** (three dots in top-right)
2. **Select "Add to Home screen"** or "Install app"
3. **Confirm installation** when prompted
4. **Find the app** on your home screen with the Ucare icon

## Uninstalling

To remove the app:
1. **Long-press** the Ucare icon on home screen
2. **Select "Uninstall"** or drag to trash
3. **Confirm removal** when prompted

The app will be completely removed from your device.
